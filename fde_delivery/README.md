# Forward Deployed Engineering (FDE) for Enterprise AI Delivery (Phase 17)

Welcome to the **Forward Deployed Engineering (FDE)** curriculum. This curriculum focuses on business discovery workshops, requirements gathering, stakeholder management, technical consulting, AI solution design, PoC validation, enterprise integration, production deployment, change management, and customer success.
