# Module 7: AI FDE Business Discovery & Stakeholder Mapping Framework

## 1. Industry Overview & Business Discovery
The **AI FDE Business Discovery Framework** is a systematic approach to identifying business goals, operational challenges, and AI opportunities.

```
+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|                                    Discovery Lifecycle                                          |
|                                                                                                 |
|   +--------------------+      +--------------------+      +--------------------+                |
|   | 1. Business Goals  | ---> | 2. Pain Points     | ---> | 3. AI Assessment   |                |
|   | (Revenue/Cost drivers)    | (Manual workflows) |      | (Automation/Agent) |                |
|   +--------------------+      +--------------------+      +---------+----------+                |
|                                                                     |                           |
|                                                                     v                           |
|                                                           +---------+----------+                |
|                                                           | 4. Solution Design |                |
|                                                           | (ROI / Value metric)                |
|                                                           +--------------------+                |
+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
```

### Discovery Phases
1.  **Define Business Goals**: Identify the client's key revenue and cost drivers.
2.  **Map Pain Points**: Walk through operational workflows with business users to identify manual processes, repetitive tasks, data challenges, and decision-making bottlenecks.
3.  **Assess AI Potential**: Evaluate identified pain points for AI suitability:
    *   **Automation**: Tasks that can be automated (e.g., parsing invoices).
    *   **Copilot**: Tasks that can be accelerated (e.g., underwriting or credit reviews).
    *   **Agent**: Complex workflows that can be orchestrated (e.g., customer service support).
    *   **Predictive Analytics**: Forecasting future states (e.g., demand forecasting or predictive maintenance).

## 2. Stakeholder Mapping
Successful AI deployments require aligning multiple stakeholder groups:
*   **Executive Sponsors (e.g. CMO, CIO)**: Define strategic goals and authorize budgets.
*   **Business Owners (e.g. Claims Manager, Plant Manager)**: Define operational metrics and evaluate solutions.
*   **Operations Teams (Business Users)**: Use the AI tools daily and provide feedback.
*   **IT & Platform Engineering Teams**: Manage integration pipelines, hosting clusters, and security policies.
*   **Compliance & Audit Teams**: Validate compliance controls (data privacy, fairness, security).

---

## 3. Business Value Measurement
To demonstrate AI solution value:
*   **Establish Baseline**: Measure metrics before deployment (e.g., 5 days to settle claims).
*   **Track Improvements**: Measure metrics after deployment (e.g., 2 hours to settle claims).
*   **Calculate ROI**: Compare operational cost savings to the implementation and hosting costs.

---

## 4. AI FDE Perspective

### Speaking the Language of Business
As an AI Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE), you act as the bridge between technical models and business outcomes.
*   Avoid technical jargon (like parameters, activation functions, or metrics scores) when speaking with business users.
*   Focus on business outcomes (cycle time reductions, accuracy improvements, and cost savings) during workshops and presentations.
