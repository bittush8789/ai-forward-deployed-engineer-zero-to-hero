# Module 9: AI FDE Consulting & Business Domain Interview Preparation

This module compiles deep business domain questions and model answers for AI Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE) interviews.

---

## 1. Insurance Domain

### Q1: What is the combined ratio, and why is it critical for insurers?
*   **Answer**: The combined ratio is the sum of the loss ratio (claims paid divided by premiums earned) and the expense ratio (operating expenses divided by premiums written). It measures underwriting profitability. A combined ratio < 100% indicates that the insurer is making an underwriting profit, while a ratio > 100% indicates they are paying out more in claims and expenses than they collect in premiums.

### Q2: How can AI help optimize the combined ratio?
*   **Answer**: AI can improve the loss ratio by automating fraud detection and enhancing underwriting accuracy (minimizing bad risk payouts), and reduce the expense ratio by automating claims intake and processing workflows.

## 2. Banking Domain

### Q1: What is Net Interest Margin (NIM), and what factors influence it?
*   **Answer**: Net Interest Margin is the difference between interest income earned on loans and interest paid to depositors, divided by total earning assets. It measures banking profitability and is influenced by interest rate environments, credit loan demand, and deposit competition.

### Q2: How can an AI Loan Copilot improve bank operations?
*   **Answer**: An AI Loan Copilot automates document parsing (tax returns, bank statements), debt-to-income calculations, and compliance checks, reducing loan approval cycles and allowing the bank to capture customers faster.

## 3. Healthcare Domain

### Q1: What are EMR and EHR systems, and how do they differ?
*   **Answer**: EMR (Electronic Medical Record) systems are digital charts used within a single clinic or hospital. EHR (Electronic Health Record) systems are designed to share patient data across different providers and institutions, offering a unified medical history.

### Q2: What security standards must be enforced when deploying AI in hospitals?
*   **Answer**: Deployed AI solutions must comply with HIPAA regulations, which require encrypting patient health information (PHI) in transit and at rest, implementing role-based access controls (RBAC), and logging all database access events.

## 4. Retail, Manufacturing & Supply Chain

### Q1: Explain Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) in manufacturing.
*   **Answer**: OEE measures machine productivity and is calculated as: Availability (active uptime) x Performance (execution speed) x Quality (good units produced divided by total units). An OEE of 100% indicates perfect production.

### Q2: What is On-Time In-Full (OTIF) in supply chain operations?
*   **Answer**: OTIF is the percentage of deliveries made within the scheduled window containing all ordered items. It measures logistics and supply chain delivery quality.

## 5. Discovery & Consulting Frameworks

### Q1: How do you identify AI opportunities during a business discovery workshop?
*   **Answer**: Review current workflows with business users to map pain points (manual tasks, data challenges, decisions bottlenecks), evaluate those processes for automation or copilot suitability, and calculate potential operational cost savings to determine priority.
